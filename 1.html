About Me

Hello, I am Maraya Carbajal, a senior at Canoga Park High School. This website is intended for updates on my second year High School Involvement (H.I.P) project with Northrop Grumman.

- I am in the High School Involvement Program (H.I.P) with Northrop Grumman Corporation.

- The Boys and Girls Club has always encouraged me to be a leader. I am now president of Keystone,their leadership club. As vice president, I help increase student awareness of issues affecting our community and lead other students in activities to address these issues.

- Captain of the Canoga Park High School Robotics Team.

- District President of the Canoga Park High School Skills USA.

- While in college, I plan to assist girls interested in STEM by creating a club which provides access and opportunities for low-income girls in K-12 by providing technological experiences, college preparation, and career awareness. I have been welcomed into the Northrop Grumman program, which has granted me the opportunity to expand my knowledge and utilize my skills to help young girls pursue careers in STEM.

- I plan to major in mechanical or aerospace engineering because I aspire to work for NASA and Northrup Grumman to develop rovers and satellites to explore new galaxies. As an aerospace engineer, I hope to contribute to aerospace research and technological advancements. My exposure to robotics in high school has helped me develop my understanding of the importance of technology in furthering science education.

- I am always curious to learn new things both because education is a great equalizer and I simply love learning.
